Hungry Cat

This cat is hungry and needs your help to get its food, and avoid enemies.

Object of the game is to walk this hungry cat around, using the arrow keys, to help it get its fishes. Cats love that.

Avoid the dogs, they are the cat's enemies!

Warning: These dogs can jump over the counter

You must get 100 points to pass each level!
